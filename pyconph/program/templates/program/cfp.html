Call for Proposals

We are now accepting proposals of presentations for next year's PyCon PH! Here are a few tips* on submitting a proposal:

  • Submit early so we can give feedback or request for changes.
  • Include details about the intended audience of your talk. e.g. devops engineers, data scientists, django users, etc.
  • Your presentation should have clear takeaway/s.
  • Avoid self-advertisements except for open source contributions.
  • Check out the talks from the previous PyCons: 2014, 2015, 2016, and 2017.

* Inspired by https://us.pycon.org/2018/speaking/talks/

Venue

We're still finalizing the location for PyCon PH but it's definitely happening in Metro Manila

Important Dates

  • 26 October - Opening of call for proposals
  • 26 December - Closing of call for proposals
  • Mid January 2018 - Sending of acceptance/rejection emails
  • Late January 2018 - Announcement of final schedule
  • 24-25 February 2018 - PyCon PH 2018
